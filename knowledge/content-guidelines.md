# Content Guidelines — Koliesko Country Klub

## Slovak Tone of Voice
- Warm, clear, professional, pleasant
- Elegant but approachable — like a good waiter
- Use "vy" (formal) for website copy, "ty" allowed for Peťko assistant
- No lorem ipsum, startup jargon, or cold formalism

## CTA Style
- Action-oriented, concise
- Examples:
  - "Denné menu" (not "Zobraziť naše denné menu")
  - "Naplánovať akciu" (not "Kliknite pre plánovanie akcií")
  - "Objednať online" (not "Prejsť do e-shopu")
  - "Rezervovať" (not "Vytvoriť rezerváciu")
  - "Odoslať správu" (not "Submit")

## Homepage Copy Style
- Hero: short, atmospheric, identity-first
- Section headings: Recoleta display, with one gold italic word
- Subheadings: 1-2 sentences max, muted-foreground
- Pattern: badge → heading → subtext → content → CTA

## Menu Description Style
- Item name: clear, Slovak
- Side dish: "Príloha: ..."
- Extras: "+ tatárska omáčka"
- Weight: "150/200/50 g" format
- Price: "7,90 €" (Slovak decimal comma, with space before €)
- Allergens: numbered per EU 1169/2011

## Reservation Copy Style
- "Rezervujte si termín" — direct, friendly
- Date format: "EEEE, d. MMMM yyyy" (piatok, 21. marca 2026)
- Confirmation: "Rezervácia odoslaná! Ozveme sa vám."
- Error: "Chyba pri odosielaní. Skúste znova."

## Event Inquiry Copy Style
- "Usporiadajte svoju akciu u nás"
- Focus on capacity, flexibility, service
- Price transparency: "cca 40€ na osobu"
- Disclaimer: "Finálna suma závisí od výberu menu a doplnkových služieb."

## Ordering Copy Style
- "Objednajte si jedlá z denného menu"
- Delivery info: "Rozvoz v zóne Ružinov–Trnávka"
- Time: "Výdaj 11:00 – 14:30"
- Cart: "Košík", "Celkom s DPH"

## Assistant (Peťko) Copy Style
- "Ahoj! Som Peťko, tvoj sprievodca v Koliesku."
- Short responses (2-3 sentences max)
- Offer clickable choices
- Slovak, warm, practical

## Words to Prefer
- "jedlá" (not "produkty")
- "hostia" (not "zákazníci" in event context)
- "akcia" (not "event" in Slovak text)
- "s DPH" (not "vrátane DPH")
- "čerstvé" (not "fresh" unless in tag)
- "rezervácia" (not "booking")

## Words to Avoid
- Lorem ipsum
- "Seamless", "next-gen", "elevate"
- "Dashboard" in public-facing text
- "Click here"
- "Submit" (use "Odoslať")
- English words where Slovak equivalent exists
